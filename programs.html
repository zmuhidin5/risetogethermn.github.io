Our Programs

Four pillars of impact — built for and by our community.

Program 01

Mentorship

Every young person deserves someone in their corner. Our mentorship program pairs youth with caring, experienced adults from similar backgrounds who understand the challenges they face.

Mentors meet regularly with youth to offer guidance, accountability, and encouragement — helping them navigate school, set goals, and build the confidence to pursue their futures.

  • One-on-one and group mentoring sessions
  • Goal setting and personal development
  • College and career pathway guidance

Program 02

Leadership Development

Tomorrow's community leaders are in our programs today. We give young people real opportunities to lead — running events, facilitating workshops, and taking ownership of initiatives that matter to their communities.

Through hands-on experience and structured training, youth develop the skills, voice, and mindset to lead with purpose.

  • Youth-led workshops and events
  • Public speaking and communication skills
  • Leadership training and civic engagement

Program 03

Sports & Recreation

Sports are more than a game — they are a classroom. Our sports and recreation program uses athletics to build discipline, teamwork, and healthy habits while keeping youth active, connected, and off the sidelines of life.

We create a safe, competitive, and fun environment where young people can channel their energy and grow as individuals and teammates.

  • Organized leagues and recreational play
  • Teamwork and sportsmanship development
  • Health and wellness education

Program 04

Community Events

We believe that strong communities are built one gathering at a time. Our community events bring families, youth, and neighbors together to celebrate culture, share resources, and strengthen the bonds that hold us together.

From cultural celebrations to community clean-ups, our events create spaces where everyone belongs and everyone contributes.

  • Cultural celebrations and family events
  • Community resource fairs
  • Volunteer and service days
